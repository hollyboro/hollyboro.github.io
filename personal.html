I spend most of my free time outdoors. I love to seek out remote areas that cannot be reached by car. Mountain biking is my primary way to do this, but I also enjoy trail running and rock climbing.

Over the past year, bikepacking has become one of my favorite ways to access the backcountry. In 2015 I bikepacked the Kokopelli trail and the Colorado trail, and I hope to take similar trips in the future.
