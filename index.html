Welcome!

I am a PhD candidate in the Aerospace Engineering Sciences department at the University of Colorado, Boulder. My advisors are Professors Jason Marden and Eric Frew.

Current Research

My research focuses on game theory applied to distributed control systems. In particular, I am interested in convergence rates and the role of information in the control of multi-agent systems. You can find my publications here.

Background

I received my B.S. degree in Mathematics in 2004 from the US Air Force Academy. After graduating, I served in the Air Force as an aircraft maintenance officer for the Global Hawk UAS and then as a deputy mission manager for the STP-S26 space launch. You can find my CV here.
